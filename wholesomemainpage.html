Wholesome Content Only

Roll two dice to choose a number. You can play one of two ways:

  • Each person rolls the dice and hits the button based on their roll. In this system, prompts can be repeated and you can stay in one mode for as long as you would like.
  • When someone rolls the dice, everyone in the circle completes the prompt as shown. In this system, if you re-roll a number, you roll again. Leave this category when all prompts have been completed.
How you play is up to you! Once the prompt is done, pass the dice to the person on your left and roll again. You may get out of answering anything/completing a task by drinking 3 times.
